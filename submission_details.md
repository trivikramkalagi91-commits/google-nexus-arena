I just wrapped up a high-stakes engineering journey: trying to transform the stadium experience for IPL 2026. 🚀

What started as a simple idea evolved into **Google Nexus Arena**—a companion app project that aims to *know* who you are and where you're sitting to make match days seamless.

### 💡 Key Technical Highlights:
- **Personalized Onboarding**: I implemented a flow that captures fan identity and ticket details to power a unique journey for every user.
- **Stadium Geometry Engine**: I built a logic-driven SVG engine to render accurate digital twins for any venue on the fly—from the oval of Narendra Modi Stadium to the circles of Chepauk.
- **Actionable Intelligence**: Real-time turn-by-turn navigation that directs you to your specific gate and seat, synced with simulated crowd density heatmaps.
- **Modern Tech Stack**: Built with React, Vite, and Framer Motion, and deployed via GitHub Pages for easy access.

Building this with **Google Antigravity** has been an incredible deep dive into agentic coding. It was a challenge to move beyond basic code and try building a complex, interconnected system for real-world logistics.
